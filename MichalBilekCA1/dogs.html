Dogs for adoption:

Welcome to our "Dogs for Adoption" page! Here, we're committed to finding loving homes for our furry friends. Each dog in our care is unique, with its own story, personality, and set of needs. We believe there's a perfect match for every dog and every prospective pet parent. Browse through our list of adorable canines waiting for their forever home. Could one of these loyal companions be your new family member?

Baxter - The Energetic Labrador Retriever

Age: 2 Years
Colour: Golden

Baxter is a lively and affectionate Labrador Retriever with a golden coat that shines in the sun. At 2 years old, he's full of energy and loves outdoor activities. Baxter enjoys long walks, playing fetch, and has a deep love for water. He is great with children and other pets, making him the perfect addition to any family. Baxter is up-to-date on all vaccinations and is microchipped.

Daisy - The Gentle Beagle

Age: 5 Years
Colour: Tri-color

Meet Daisy, a gentle and loving 5-year-old Beagle with a beautiful tri-color coat. She has a calm demeanor and enjoys leisurely strolls and cozy cuddle sessions. Daisy is an ideal companion for those seeking a quieter, more laid-back pet. She's friendly with people of all ages and gets along well with other dogs. Daisy is spayed, microchipped, and fully vaccinated.

Rocky - The Playful Boxer Mix

Age: 3 Years
Colour: Brindle

Rocky is a 3-year-old Boxer mix with a striking brindle coat and an infectious spirit of playfulness. He's a bundle of joy and energy, always ready for a game or an adventure. He is very intelligent and responds well to training. Rocky is great with older children and would make a wonderful and loyal companion. He is neutered, microchipped, and up-to-date on his vaccinations.
